About Oceanside's Favorite Ramen Stop

After a vacation to South Korea, owner Sandy opened Corner Ramen as a one-stop-shop for all those last minute lunches, busy bees, cute first dates and relaxing get-togethers with friends. We have all your fave ramen, noodles, beers and snacks...come microwave a bowl and just relax.
